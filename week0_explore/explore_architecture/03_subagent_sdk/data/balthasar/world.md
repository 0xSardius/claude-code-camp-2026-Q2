# World Map Notes — Balthasar

## Known rooms (Midgaard)

- **The Temple Of Midgaard** — starting room for new characters. Donation
  room to the east (one-time-ish free starter gear from "a kind soul" NPC,
  confirmed useful).
- **Donation room** (east of Temple Of Midgaard) — free starter gear NPC.
- **Main Street (west segment)** — south -> Guild of Magic Users entrance,
  north -> magic shop (unvisited), west -> Inside The West Gate Of
  Midgaard, east -> continues along Main Street toward Armory/bakery
  segment.
- **Main Street (middle segment, Armory/bakery)** — south -> Armory
  entrance, north -> bakery, east -> Market Square, west -> back toward
  Guild of Magic Users segment.
- **Inside The West Gate Of Midgaard** — cityguard here (non-hostile,
  guarding). Exits: e (back to Main Street), s (Wall Road), w (Outside The
  West Gate).
- **Outside The West Gate Of Midgaard** — edge of a big forest to the
  west. Exits: e (back inside gate), w (into forest — unexplored, likely
  a *different* zone, not the Newbie Zone). DEAD-END-ish for our purposes.
- **Wall Road** — next to the western city wall, road continues south.
  Exits: n (back to inside-gate room), s (unexplored). Probably leads
  around the city wall, not obviously toward Newbie Zone — deprioritized.
- **Market Square** — the central hub. Exits: n -> Temple Square, s ->
  Common Square (unexplored), e/w -> Main Street segments.
- **The Temple Square** — marble steps up to a temple gate to the north.
  Fountain here. Exits: n (temple gate — UNEXPLORED, most promising lead
  toward Newbie Zone per hints), w -> Clerics' Guild, e -> Grunting Boar
  Inn, s -> Market Square.
- **Guild of Magic Users** — south from the west segment of Main Street;
  guildmaster here takes `practice magic missile`.
